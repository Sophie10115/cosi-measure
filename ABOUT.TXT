COSI Measure is an Open Source 3D Multipurpose Measurement System with submillimetre 
fidelity. It has been developed at the Berlin Ultrahigh Field Facility (B.U.F.F.) 
of the Max Delbrück Center for Molecular Medicine, Berlin, Germany. The documentation 
for the hardware and the software is made freely available to the community. 

This work is part of the Open Source Imaging Initiative (www.opensourceimaging.org)
and was supported in part by the Federal Ministry for Education and Research (BMBF, 
KMU innovativ Medizintechnik, 3-in-1 THERAHEAT, FKZ 13GW0102A and 13GW0102B).
